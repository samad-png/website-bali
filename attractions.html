Top Attractions in Bali

Bali is home to numerous captivating attractions. Here are some of the must-visit spots:

  • Sacred Monkey Forest: A lush sanctuary filled with hundreds of playful monkeys.
  • Uluwatu Temple: A cliff-top temple offering stunning ocean views.
  • Tegallalang Rice Terraces: The scenic, green terraces perfect for a tranquil walk.
